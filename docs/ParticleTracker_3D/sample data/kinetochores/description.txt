The file Cell1_5frames shows kinetochores in a humen cell in the beginning of a mitosis. The kinetochores were marked
with a GFP. The image was sampled down to 8 bit. The movie was recorded by the group of Prof. Patrick Meraldi at the institute of Biochemistry at the ETHZ.
pixel width = 110nm
pixel height = 110nm
voxel depth = 500nm
focal planes = 25
