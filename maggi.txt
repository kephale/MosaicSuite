In a micro oven proved bowl, take a cup of hot water ( half cup more than what you take while cooking in gas oven) crunch the maggie into piece.
Cut the masala packet. Stir a well so that the maggi dips in water well.
Then put the bowl in micro oven. Set to micro oven mode. Set time of 5 minutes. And push start.
After 5 minutes, serve the maggi in a plate.
You are ready for the yummy maggi break fast.
